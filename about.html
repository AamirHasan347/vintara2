OUR STORY

It started when I switched to jute bags to cut down on daily plastic use. What felt like a small act soon sparked a bigger question: Why not bring this eco-friendly solution to more people?

Jute wasn’t just eco-friendly – it had character. It was strong, reusable, and reflected values I deeply believed in: sustainability, responsibility, and conscious living.

That belief led to the birth of Vintara International – a space where nature meets intention. Every product we create is a reminder that daily choices matter, and that beauty doesn’t have to come at the Earth’s expense.

We’re not here just to sell bags, we’re here to build a community that chooses better. For themselves. For the planet.

Our Vision

Choosing jute bags began as a small step toward reducing waste but soon grew into a passion for meaningful, eco-conscious living. Their reusable, earth-friendly design revealed how small choices can drive real impact.

Today, through Vintara, we bring this vision to brands, hotels, and businesses across the world and beyond — helping them embrace sustainable packaging that speaks to both the planet and their people.

Our Mission

At Vintara, our mission is to craft premium jute products that help you to express your identity with style and purpose. We believe packaging isn’t just a container — it’s a silent ambassador of values.

By blending sustainability, customization, and conscious design, we aim to offer solutions that elevate brands, hotels, and businesses while supporting the planet, skilled artisans, and ethical production practices.
